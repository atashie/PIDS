# Acquisition

## 1. Purpose
Flight planning and field procedures that turn a site into usable raw data: flight lines, altitude/overlap (ground sampling distance), and ground control (RTK/PPK GNSS, ground control points) for georeferencing accuracy.

- Outputs: raw georeferenced captures → processing.
